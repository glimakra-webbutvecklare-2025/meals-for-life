Ingredients

  • 1 dl rice
  • 8 dl broth
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Optional toppings: sliced green onions, soy sauce, cooked chicken, soft boiled egg, sesame oil, fresh ginger

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water, the rice does not need to run clear since we want to keep some starch.
  2. In a large pot, bring the broth to a boil. Add the rice and salt.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for about 1 to 1.5 hours, or until the rice breaks down and the congee reaches your desired consistency. You can add more broth or water if it becomes too thick. Make sure to monitor the rice so it does not burn!
  4. Serve hot with your choice of toppings such as sliced green onions, soy sauce, cooked chicken, a soft boiled egg, sesame oil, or fresh ginger.
